Alnö is called Norrlands Hawaii because it is an popular island, just outside Sundsvalls town that have a lot of beaches and summer vibes. Krokviken is a perfect place to stay in. It is a calm, privat beach and is only 2 kilometers away from bigger beaches of both sides with activities as beach volly net, yoga classes etc. It is also located in bottom of a hill with a wonderful view. You can buy smoked withefish diretly from the neighbor, pick raspberrry and blueberrys from the garden to your breakfast smoothie or chantarelles in the forest just across the street.
