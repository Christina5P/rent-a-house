Alnö is called Norrlands Hawaii because it is an popular island, just outside Sundsvalls town.

The island have a lot of beaches and summer vibes.
Krokviken is a perfect place to stay in. It has a calm, privat beach and is only 2 kilometers away from bigger beaches of both sides with activities as beachvolly net, yoga classes etc.
It is also located in bottom of a hill with a wonderful view.
You can buy smoked withefish diretly from the neighbor, pick raspberrry and blueberrys to your breakfast smoothie from the garden or pick chantarelles in the forest just across the street.
